LC8_SR-1

Warning

This product is deprecated and not available anymore in BDC. LC8_SR-1 is being replaced by LANDSAT_SR-2, which consists in Landsat Collection-2 instead of Collection 1 and will also contain Landsat-9 images.

Brazil Data Cube Team.

The LC8_SR-1 image collection product contains data from the image collection LC8_DN-1 processed to Surface Reflectance data at its native spatial resolution through LaSRC [21].
