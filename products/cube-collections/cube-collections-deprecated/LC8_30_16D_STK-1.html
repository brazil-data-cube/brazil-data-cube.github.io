LC8_30_16D_STK-1

Warning

This product is being deprecated and will not be available anymore. LC8_30_16D_STK-1 is being replaced by LANDSAT-2M-1. This new product will use Landsat Collection-2 instead of Collection 1, will generated using BDC GRID newest version (version 2) and will also be generated using Landsat-9 images. The LC8_30_16D_STK-1 product will be available until we process the same areas and times in the new product.

Brazil Data Cube Team.

The LC8_30_16D_STK-1 product contains OLI surface reflectance at full spatial resolution (30m) and 16 days Temporal Compositing considering the FMASK cloud mask. The following Table lists the LC8_30_16D_STK-1 product characteristics:
