.. _totalob:

TOTALOB
-------


BDC TOTALOB band indicates the total number of observations during the composed interval, according to the composed images cloud mask. Considering a 16 days composed data cube, if the TOTALOB indicates the value 3 in a specific pixel, this means that during the 16 days composition period, three images presented valid observations.
