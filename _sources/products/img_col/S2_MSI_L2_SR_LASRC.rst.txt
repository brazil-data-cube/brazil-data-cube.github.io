S2_L2A_LASRC
------------


The S2_L2A_LASRC image collection product contains data from the image collection :doc:`S2_L1C <./S2_L1C>` processed to Surface Reflectance data at its native spatial resolution through *LaSRC* :cite:`vermote:2016` version 2.0.1.
