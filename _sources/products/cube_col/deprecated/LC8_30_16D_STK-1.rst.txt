LC8_30_16D_STK-1
----------------

.. warning::

    This product is being deprecated and will soon not be available anymore. LC8_30_16D_STK-1 is being replaced by :doc:`LANDSAT-2M-1 <./../LANDSAT-2M-1>`. This new product will use Landsat Collection-2 instead of Collection 1, will generated using BDC GRID newest version (version 2) and will also be generated using Landsat-9 images.
    The LC8_30_16D_STK-1 product will be available until we process the same areas and times in the new product.

    Brazil Data Cube Team.
