S2_10
-----


The S2_10 product contains MSI surface reflectance at full spatial resolution (10m) and the satellite sensor original Temporal Resolution (Temporal Compositing identity), which implies images at most each 5 days when Sentinel-2A and Sentinel-2B are operating together, after March 2017, and each 10 days before.
