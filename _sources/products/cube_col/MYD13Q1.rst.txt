MYD13Q1
-------


The MYD13Q1 collection product contains AQUA/MODIS Surface Reflectance data at its native spatial resolution (250m). These images are the same that can be found at NASA's Earth Date (https://search.earthdata.nasa.gov/search) or USGS's Earth Explorer (https://earthexplorer.usgs.gov/) catalogs.
