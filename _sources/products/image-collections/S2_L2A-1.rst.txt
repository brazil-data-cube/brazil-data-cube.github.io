S2_L2A-1
--------


The S2_L2A-1 image collection product contains data from the image collection :doc:`S2_L1C-1 <./image-collection-deprecated/S2_L1C-1>` processed to Surface Reflectance data at its native spatial resolution through *Sen2cor* :cite:`louis:2016`, converted to GeoTiff, instead of JPEG2000.
