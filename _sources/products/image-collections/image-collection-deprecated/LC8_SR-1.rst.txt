LC8_SR-1
--------

.. warning::

    This product is deprecated and not available anymore in BDC. LC8_SR-1 is being replaced by :doc:`LANDSAT_SR-2 <./LANDSAT_SR-2>`, which consists in Landsat Collection-2 instead of Collection 1 and will also contain Landsat-9 images.

    Brazil Data Cube Team.


The LC8_SR-1 image collection product contains data from the image collection :doc:`LC8_DN-1 <./LC8_DN-1>` processed to Surface Reflectance data at its native spatial resolution through LaSRC :cite:`vermote:2016`.
