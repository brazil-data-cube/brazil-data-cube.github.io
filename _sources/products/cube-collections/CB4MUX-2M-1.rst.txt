CB4MUX_2-1
----------

The CB4MUX_2-1 product contains MUX surface reflectance at full spatial resolution (20m) and 1 month Temporal Compositing applied over the CBERS-4/MUX data considering the :doc:`CMASK <../../specifications/bands/CMASK>` cloud mask. The following Table lists the CB4MUX_2-1 product characteristics:
