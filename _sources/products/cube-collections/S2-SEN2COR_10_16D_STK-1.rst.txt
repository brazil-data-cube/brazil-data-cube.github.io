S2-SEN2COR_10_16D_STK-1
-----------------------


.. warning::

    This product is being deprecated and will not be available anymore. S2-SEN2COR_10_16D_STK-1 was replaced by :doc:`S2-16D-2 <./S2-16D-2>`. The new product was generated using BDC GRID newest version (version 2).

    Brazil Data Cube Team.
