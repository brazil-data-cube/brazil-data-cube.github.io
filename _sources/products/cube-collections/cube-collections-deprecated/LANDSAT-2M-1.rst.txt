LANDSAT-2M-1
------------

.. warning::

    This product is deprecated and not be available anymore. LANDSAT-2M-1 is being replaced by :doc:`LANDSAT-16D-1 <./../LANDSAT-16D-1>`.

    Brazil Data Cube Team.
