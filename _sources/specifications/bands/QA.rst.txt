.. _qa:

QA
--


Quality Assessment (QA) bands provide, for Landsat Collection 2, useful information to users regarding conditions of each pixel observation. A more detailed description of this band can be found on https://www.usgs.gov/landsat-missions/landsat-collection-2-quality-assessment-bands.
