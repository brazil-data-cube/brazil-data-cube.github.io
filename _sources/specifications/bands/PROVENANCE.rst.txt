.. _provenance:

PROVENANCE
----------


BDC PROVENANCE band indicates the origin date of the image selected as best pixel of the composed interval. Its values range from 1 to 366, where 1 represents January first and 366 December 31 (in leap years). Based on that, if the PROVENANCE band indicates the value 231 for a specific pixel, this means that the value came from an image of August 19. The following tables shows Julian date calendars used by the PROVENANCE band:
