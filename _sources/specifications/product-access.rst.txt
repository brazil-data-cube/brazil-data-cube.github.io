BDC Product Access
------------------

You can access the collections provided by BDC on [INPE's STAC Browser](https://data.inpe.br/stac/browser/?.language=en), directly on [INPE's STAC](https://data.inpe.br/bdc/stac/v1/) or through BDC Explorer (http://brazildatacube.dpi.inpe.br/portal/explore).

    .. Note:: We are migrating all BDC collections to INPE's STAC. In the meantime, a few collections can be found on BDC STAC old URL (http://brazildatacube.dpi.inpe.br/stac/).
